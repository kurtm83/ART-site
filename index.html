About

I have been using Rhino 3D since 2013, primarily in the theater, set design, exhibit, and display industries. I am certified by McNeel as an Authorized Rhino Trainer. I come from a construction background and my primary focus and skillset is using Rhino in the AEC industries, using blocks, layers, layouts, and exporting CNC files.

My new area of focus is in using Grasshopper, Python, and custom macros to automate workflows. I'd be happy to work with your team, to take a look at your current setup and see how we can leverage the power of Rhino and computational design to improve efficiency and reduce redundancy.

  • One-on-one Rhino training tailored to your needs.
  • Group training for companies and teams.
  • Consultation on Rhino workflows and automation.
